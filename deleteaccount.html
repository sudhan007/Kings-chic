Delete My Account

If you wish to delete your King's Chick account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Account Settings" section.
  3. Scroll down and click on the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Please note that once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All your data, including order history and saved preferences, will be permanently removed from our system.

If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact us at support@kingschick.com.
