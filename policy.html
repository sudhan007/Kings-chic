Privacy Policy

At Prathap Construction, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for our service, we collect your name, email address, and delivery address. We also collect information about the clothes you submit for ironing, such as the type of garment, fabric, and any special instructions or preferences.

In addition, we may collect information about your device, such as the IP address, browser type, and operating system, to improve our services and enhance your user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to process your laundry and ironing orders, communicate with you about your orders, and deliver your freshly pressed clothes back to you. We may also use your information to improve our services, conduct internal analytics, respond to customer inquiries or requests, and send you promotional offers or updates about PressUp.

If you have consented to receive marketing communications from us, we may use your information to send you newsletters, special offers, or other promotional materials related to PressUp's services. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails or by contacting us directly.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers and partners who assist us in operating our business and providing our services. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, to comply with a legal obligation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of PressUp, our customers, or others.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption technologies, firewalls, and other security protocols to safeguard your data. We also regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the ongoing protection of your information.

However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a security breach occurs, we will take appropriate steps to mitigate the situation and notify you if required by applicable laws and regulations.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or by contacting us directly.

If you have any concerns about our use of your personal information or would like to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@prathap.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates. If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or through a prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@prathap.com. We will be happy to assist you.
