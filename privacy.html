Privacy Policy

At King's Chick, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address. We also collect details about your order, including the items purchased, delivery preferences, and payment details.

Additionally, we may collect information about your device, such as the IP address, browser type, and operating system, to improve our services and enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to process your orders, prepare and deliver your meals, and communicate with you regarding your orders. We may also use your information to:

  • Enhance your customer experience
  • Analyze trends and improve our services
  • Send you promotional offers or updates about King's Chick

You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails or by contacting us directly.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering your orders and operating our business. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and cannot use your information for other purposes.

We may also disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights, property, or safety of King's Chick, our customers, or others.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. While we strive to use the best means to protect your data, no method is 100% secure. In the event of a security breach, we will notify you as required by law.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. For assistance, contact us at privacy@kingschick.com. You may also request to stop receiving marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, contact us at privacy@kingschick.com. We're here to help!
